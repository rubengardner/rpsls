Instructions

This is an extended version of the clasical "Rock, Paper & Scissors" game. In this version there are 5 options to choose as a weapon, adding "Lizzard" and "Spock" to the selection. The first player to win 5 rounds will become the champion! Underneath you can check all the possible outcomes in battle, be prepared, you're enemy is waiting!
